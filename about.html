One of my main interests is technology and how it affects the modern world. Technology has changed the way people communicate, learn, and work. I am especially interested in data science because it helps us analyze information and discover patterns that would otherwise be difficult to see. Learning programming and working with data allows me to better understand how systems work and how decisions can be improved through analysis.

Another area that interests me is finance and business. I enjoy learning how companies grow, raise capital, and compete in global markets. Venture capital and startups are particularly exciting topics because they involve innovation and new ideas. Many successful companies started as small ideas, and understanding how they grow helps us see how entrepreneurship shapes the economy.
