Things I Enjoy Learning About

One of my main interests is technology and how it affects the world today. Technology has changed the way people communicate, work, and learn. I am particularly interested in data science and how data can help organizations understand trends and make better decisions. Learning programming and working with data has helped me develop problem solving skills and a deeper understanding of how information can be transformed into useful insights.

Another interest of mine is finance and business. I enjoy learning how companies grow, raise capital, and compete in global markets. Topics like venture capital, startups, and innovation are especially exciting to me because they show how new ideas can turn into successful businesses. I believe that combining knowledge from both data science and finance can open many opportunities to analyze markets and help companies make strategic decisions.
