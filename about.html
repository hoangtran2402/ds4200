Things I Enjoy Learning About

One of my main interests is photography. I enjoy photography because it allows me to capture moments, environments, and perspectives that might otherwise be overlooked. Through a camera, I can pay closer attention to lighting, composition, and details that make an image visually interesting. Photography has also helped me become more observant and creative when exploring new places.

In addition, photography allows me to document experiences when traveling or exploring different environments. Whether it is capturing landscapes, city scenes, or everyday moments, photography gives me a way to tell visual stories. I especially enjoy experimenting with angles, lighting, and framing to create images that feel natural and engaging.
